Nobleserve Regulation
and Governance

Nobleserve Finance is a licensed Finance Company regulated by the Central Bank of Nigeria. We adhere to the Code of Corporate Governance for Banks and Other Financial Institutions, as well as the Bank and Other Financial Institutions Acts.

Our operations are guided by a robust risk management framework, ensuring the identification, measurement, monitoring, and control of risks. Additionally, we have an internal Code of Conduct that upholds the highest standards of professional behavior, business conduct, and sustainable practices.

Nobleserve Finance, in order to further commit ourselves to corporate governance, is a registered member of the following self-regulatory and professional Associations:Finance House Association of Nigeria(FHAN) Equipment Leasing Association of Nigeria(ELAN)
